Eggs In Purgatory
Kale and Sausage Soup

Instructions

  1. In a large pan add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic, simmer until fragrant.
  2. Add jar of stewed tomatoes and crushed red pepper flakes, keep on low heat until bubbling.
  3. Optional * add hot sauce.
  4. Put in as many eggs as desired then place a lid on top until eggs are poached.
  5. Serve on Garlic Bread and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Fresh Bread
  • Stewed Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Hot Sauce (optional)
