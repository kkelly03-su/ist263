Eggs In Purgatory

Instructions

  1. In a large pan add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic, simmer until fragrant.
  2. Add jar of stewed tomatoes and crushed red pepper flakes, keep on low heat until bubbling.
  3. Optional * add hot sauce.
  4. Put in as many eggs as desired then place a lid on top until eggs are poached.
  5. Serve on Garlic Bread and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Fresh Bread
  • Stewed Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Hot Sauce (optional)

Kale and Sausage Soup

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, put 2 tablespoons of olive oil, fresh garlic, salt, and pepper. Simmer until fragrant. Then add minced carrots, celery, and white onion until they are saut√©ed.
  2. In a separate frying pan, cook sausage until brown.
  3. Add water to the pot with chicken bouillon powder and stir.
  4. Add cooked sausage and kale.
  5. Add 2 bay leaves and other spices to taste.
  6. Squeeze in half a lemon.
  7. Optional: add orzo or rice.
  8. Stir and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Sausage
  • Chicken Bouyon Powder
  • Lemon
  • Carrots
  • White Onion
  • Celery
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Basil
  • Bay Leaves
  • Basmati Rice or Orzo (optional)
